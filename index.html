ABOUT THE EVENT

🏛️ About NSCET

Founded under Theni Melapettai Hindu Nadargal Uravinmurai, NSCET is an AICTE approved institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Accredited with NAAC 'A' Grade, ISO 9001:2015 certified, and recognized under UGC Section 2(f). Located at Vadapudupatti, Annanji PO, Theni – 625 531.


Motto: "Engineering Excellence for Empowerment"

🧠 7th Sense Association

The elite student association of the Department of Science and Humanities, NSCET. Dedicated to organizing technical events, workshops, and hackathons designed to develop holistic engineering skills and foster innovation among first-year scholars.

⚔️ Next Gen Ninjas

A rigorous 6-hour intra-college hackathon featuring 50 real-world problem statements (25 Software, 25 Hardware). Teams of up to 6 members, guided by a faculty mentor, will race against time to architect and prototype innovative solutions under intense pressure.
